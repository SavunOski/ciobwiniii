Corruption Is Only Bad if I'm Not Involved In It (CIOBWINIII)

This is a Proof-of-Concept game about being corrupt. 
You, as the friend in high places, are tasked with helping your unlicensed uncle keep his backwater clinic operational.

The GAMSPy mathematical model tries its hardest to work in the citizens' favor and optimize paths to hospitals,
and you, as the president, try to optimize only for the clinic to make sure only you and your friends in court profit even at the cost of citizens' lives.
But beware, each day it gets harder and harder to run your Uncle's clinic (denoted by the star emoji), so your re-optimization has to be perfect!
This is an optimization race between human and machine. With the power of political corruption in your favor, can you win?
